Computer Science: Synonym Identifier

For a school project, I developed a Python program that identifies the best synonym for a given word from a list of choices. Depending on the similarity algorithm used, the program reached 75% accuracy.

Design Project: Litter Collection

As part of a design course, I worked with a group of 4 to design an improved apparatus for manual litter collection on the streets of Toronto. Focusing on durability and ergonomics, we redesigned the handle, added an extendable pole, and developed a ingenious collection plate that allows user customization without causing wear.
